## Hayat Care Centers — Business Brief (for image generation context)

### 1) What this website is for
Hayat Care Centers is a senior-care business website. It presents Hayat as a care provider that helps older adults and their families through supportive daily services focused on dignity, safety, and quality of life.

### 2) Core business offerings
Hayat promotes three connected service lines:

1. **Adult Care Center (daytime center-based care)**
   - Supervised daytime support
   - Structured activities and social engagement
   - Meals and wellness routines
   - Family updates and coordination

2. **Home Care Agency (in-home support)**
   - Personal care (grooming, dressing, daily routine support)
   - Companionship and check-ins
   - Meal and light housekeeping support
   - Flexible scheduling based on family needs

3. **Non-Medical Transportation**
   - Scheduled rides for appointments, errands, and community visits
   - Mobility-aware transportation support
   - Family communication around scheduling
   - Not emergency/ambulance service

### 3) Target audience
- Families caring for older adults
- Adult children making care decisions for parents
- Seniors needing daytime support, at-home support, or transportation help
- Caregivers looking for coordinated, trustworthy services

### 4) Brand positioning and tone
- Warm, welcoming, human-centered care
- Hospitality-first (not cold/clinical)
- Calm and dignified support
- Safety + independence in balance
- Strong family communication and peace of mind

### 5) Visual themes to use for AI image generation
- Bright, comfortable senior community interiors
- Caregivers helping older adults with warmth and respect
- Group activities (art, music, light movement, conversation circles)
- Shared dining moments in a friendly atmosphere
- Home-visit care scenes with familiar household settings
- Safe, professional transportation scenes for seniors
- Family consultation moments (trust, reassurance, clarity)
